Project 1

Inspired by the works of Undertale, this game follows the adventures of the silent protaganist, Eni, as they traverse their world in search of mysterious orbs with immense power.
What are these orbs for and why must they be collected? Join Eni on their arduous adventure to find out!

The game is still fairly early in development.
All pixel art by me is being drawn in Pixel Studio.
